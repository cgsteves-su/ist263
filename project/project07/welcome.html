Welcome to Cara's Closet!

Hi everyone, welcome to my closet! My whole life, I have found joy from picking out unique and special clothes for various occasions. As a college student, it can be hard to create cute and unique outfits on a budget and with little time. Despite this, I still believe that clothing is one of the best ways to show the world who you are. Believe it or not, there are so many simple ways to add personalized touches to outfits for every occasion. This page will help you discover those tricks!


On this page, you will find outfit inspiration for 3 different events:

  1. Going to class
  2. Game day
  3. Date night

I am going to give my spin on outfit ideas for these scenarios, gaining inspiration from what I see Syracuse students wearing and connecting them to popular social media trends. After reading, you will be prepared to conquer campus by showing off your personality and looking amazing doing it.
